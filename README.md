# 📊 Workforce Performance & Employee Insights Dashboard

## 🚀 Overview

An interactive **Power BI dashboard** that analyzes workforce performance, employee distribution, and training effectiveness to support **data-driven HR decisions**.

---

## 🎯 Key Highlights

* 📌 **2845 Employees** analyzed across departments, states, and demographics
* 📉 **13.6% Termination Rate** with department-level insights
* 📊 **Performance Analysis** highlighting high performers and improvement areas
* 🎯 **Training Metrics** including success rate and total cost (1.59M)
* 😊 **Engagement & Satisfaction** trends for workforce improvement

---

## 📊 Insights Delivered

* **Workforce Distribution** → Diversity & segmentation analysis
* **Department Analysis** → Production has highest workforce & attrition
* **Performance Metrics** → Majority meet expectations, clear improvement segments
* **Training Outcomes** → Completion, failure, and effectiveness tracking
* **Geographical View** → State-wise employee distribution
* **Termination Analysis** → Department & gender-based patterns
